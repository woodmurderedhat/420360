Justice

XI
Upright Meanings:

• Justice
• Truth
• Balance
• Fairness
• Cause and effect

Reversed Meanings:

• Injustice
• Dishonesty
• Imbalance
• Unfair treatment
• Lack of accountability

Element: Air
Zodiac: Libra
Color: Emerald Green
Hebrew Letter: Lamed
Sephira: Geburah/Tiphareth
Tarot Path: Path 22 on the Tree of Life

Justice holds the sword of truth in one hand and the scales of balance in the other. In Hermetic tradition, Justice represents cosmic law—the principle that every action has a consequence, every cause an effect. Libra's influence brings the need for equilibrium in all things. This is not human law with its imperfections, but divine law, perfect and impartial. The card teaches that we cannot escape the consequences of our actions, but also that we can trust in the ultimate fairness of the universe. Truth will out; balance will be restored.

Meditation Practice: The Scales of Truth

Duration: 15 minutes

Best Time: When facing decisions, or seeking clarity about right action

  1. Visualize yourself before a great figure of Justice, seated and blindfolded
  2. She holds golden scales, perfectly balanced
  3. Place on one side a situation or choice you are weighing
  4. Watch what rises on the other side as counterweight
  5. The scales show you the true cost or true value
  6. Accept the truth shown, even if uncomfortable
  7. Ask: "What is the right action here?"
  8. Listen for the answer in the stillness

Integration: Let truth guide you, not convenience. Accept responsibility for your actions. Trust that the universe maintains perfect balance, even when you cannot see it.
