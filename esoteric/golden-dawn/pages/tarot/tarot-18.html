The Moon governs the realm of dreams, intuition, and the unconscious mind. Unlike the Star's clear light, the Moon's light is reflected and diffuse‚Äîwe see by borrowed illumination. In Hermetic work, the Moon represents the gateway between consciousness and unconsciousness, the realm of symbolic truth rather than literal fact. Pisces dissolves boundaries between worlds. The card asks: What am I not seeing clearly? What illusions persist? Yet it also honors the truth of dreams, intuition, and the inner worlds. The Moon teaches that not all truth is rational‚Äîsome truths can only be perceived in darkness, through feeling and vision.

Meditation Practice: The Dream Journey

Duration: 15 minutes (best at night or before sleep)

Best Time: Before sleep or during deep introspection

  1. Lie comfortably as if preparing to sleep
  2. Visualize moonlight streaming through a window onto your bed
  3. Feel your body becoming lighter, more dream-like
  4. You float upward into a landscape of symbols and archetypes
  5. Ask a question that touches your deepest self: "What must I understand?"
  6. Observe the dream-symbols that appear without judgment
  7. Know that they carry truth, even if not literal
  8. Return slowly to waking consciousness, carrying their wisdom

Integration: Pay attention to dreams. Record them. Decipher their symbolic language. The unconscious speaks truths the waking mind refuses to hear.
