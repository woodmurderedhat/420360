Post-Postmodern Generative Study

Autonomy Flux

The field remembers pressure, then slowly returns to civic rhythm. Motion is a vote for agency. Turbulence appears, but it does not become doctrine.

This work treats autonomy as a distributed condition, not a heroic center. Each gesture perturbs the commons, but the system is tuned toward de-escalation rather than permanent crisis. It is a small refusal of inevitability.
