The Lesser Banishing Ritual

of the Pentagram (LBRP)
⚠️ Important: This is a foundational ritual of Western magic. It is taught at the Practicus grade (Initiate 1°). Before practicing, ensure you understand the Tree of Life, the four elements, and the archangelic correspondences.

The Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram is the foundation of all practical Western magic. It accomplishes four essential goals:

  • ✓ Establishes sacred space free from negative influences
  • ✓ Brings your consciousness into alignment with divine forces
  • ✓ Awakens the four elements within your being
  • ✓ Creates a magical circle protecting your work

This is not religious or supernatural. It is a psychological and energetic practice that develops consciousness and creates intentional space for spiritual work.

Preparation

⚙️ Before You Begin
  • Find a quiet space where you won't be disturbed
  • Stand facing East (the direction of air and intellect)
  • Ground yourself - feel your feet on the earth
  • Take 3 deep breaths to center consciousness
  • Have clear intention: to establish sacred space

The Ritual Steps

1 Qabalistic Cross

Touch your forehead and say: "Ateh" (Thine is)

Touch your genitals and say: "Malkuth" (The Kingdom)

Touch your right shoulder and say: "Ve-Geburah" (And the Power)

Touch your left shoulder and say: "Ve-Gedulah" (And the Glory)

Cross arms on chest and say: "Le-Olam, Amen" (Forever, Amen)

This invokes the divine light from above, grounding it in your body.

2 Banish East (Air)

Face East. Draw a pentagram in the air before you (beginning from top, down-left, up-right, down-right, up-left, back to top).

Point your finger into the center and vibrate: "ELOHIM" (The Powerful One)

Visualize the pentagram glowing yellow-gold as you trace it.

3 Banish South (Fire)

Turn South. Draw another pentagram.

Vibrate: "ADONAI" (The Lord)

Visualize this pentagram glowing red.

4 Banish West (Water)

Turn West. Draw another pentagram.

Vibrate: "EHEIEH" (I Am)

Visualize this pentagram glowing blue.

5 Banish North (Earth)

Turn North. Draw another pentagram.

Vibrate: "AGLA" (Mighty One)

Visualize this pentagram glowing green.

6 Invoke the Archangels

Return to East, arms outstretched. Say:

"Before me, Raphael (pronounced Rah-fah-EL)
Behind me, Gabriel
To my right, Michael
To my left, Uriel
And above me shines the six-rayed star"

Visualize each archangel in their corresponding direction, radiating light.

7 Close with Qabalistic Cross

Repeat the Qabalistic Cross (Step 1) to seal the space.

After the Ritual

Your sacred space is now established. You can:

  • ✓ Perform meditation or other magical work
  • ✓ Study sacred texts
  • ✓ Visualize ascents through the Tree of Life
  • ✓ Develop your magical consciousness

When finished, ground yourself by touching the earth and taking a few normal breaths. The space remains protected until you close it with the Qabalistic Cross again.

Key Points to Remember

  • ✓ Vibrate the divine names from your heart, not just your throat
  • ✓ Visualization is essential - see the light clearly
  • ✓ Intention is everything - you are not commanding the archangels but respectfully invoking them
  • ✓ Practice regularly - familiarity makes the ritual more powerful
  • ✓ Feel the presence of divine force, don't just say words

The light descends. The circle is sealed. The work begins.
