Golden Dawn Study

Adeptus Major (6=5) Practice Workbook

Daily Foundation Practices

Time Requirement: 90-120 minutes daily

  • Complete Banishing and Invoking (30 minutes)
  • Advanced Middle Pillar with Abyss work (30 minutes)
  • Magical work or healing service (30 minutes)
  • Deep meditation on Divine union (20-30 minutes)
Exercise 1: The Wand of Supreme Will

Purpose: Master the most advanced magical operations.

Practice: At this grade, you work with the highest powers of creative will. Your magical operations expand to include healing at distance, manifesting complex goals, and influencing events on the world stage. Work with utmost responsibility and always with the highest good. Every operation must be guided by your Holy Guardian Angel.

Exercise 2: Conscious Evolution

Purpose: Guide not just your own evolution but participate in humanity's spiritual evolution.

Practice: At this stage, you become a conscious participant in the Great Work of humanity. Engage in large-scale magical operations for world peace, spiritual awakening, and planetary healing. This is not egotistical—it's service. Work always under the guidance of your HGA and the ascended masters.

Exercise 3: The Palace of the King

Purpose: Attain consciousness of Chokmah (the highest consciousness below pure divinity).

Practice: Meditate on Chokmah (the Will/Fatherhood of God) and Binah (the Understanding/Motherhood of God). Understand how all creation springs from their union. Attain consciousness that includes but transcends the sense of separate self. Perform this meditation 3-4 times weekly for extended periods (30+ minutes).

Exercise 4: The Crown of All

Purpose: Prepare consciousness for the ultimate initiation—direct union with Kether (the Godhead).

Practice: Contemplate Kether—the point source of all existence, the Crown above all. Understand that it is simultaneously yourself and infinitely transcendent. This paradox cannot be resolved intellectually—it is attained through direct mystical experience. This is the goal toward which all Hermetic work points.

Focus Areas at This Grade

Magical Responsibility:

  • All magical operations guided by HGA
  • Works for healing and planetary benefit
  • Teaching and mentoring of junior adepts
  • Manifestation of spiritual light in the world

Mystical Development:

  • Regular and deep Abyss crossing work
  • Attainment of Chokmah and Binah consciousness
  • Preparation for Kether union
  • Integration of the entire Tree of Life into living experience
Exercise 5: The Great Sabbath

Purpose: Union of all opposites in the consciousness of God.

Practice: Meditate that within the Godhead (Kether), all opposites are united:

  • Light and darkness are one
  • Creation and destruction are one
  • Self and other are one
  • Being and nothingness are one
  • Male and female are one
  • All dualities dissolve in the Absolute
This is not intellectualization—it is experiential realization.

Adeptus Major Mastery

Ongoing Work:

  • Advanced magical operations (3-6 months per operation)
  • Training of the next generation of magicians
  • Deep mystical research and exploration
  • Preparation for the Exemptus grade (the grade of solitude)
  • Work toward union with the Divine

Duration: Adeptus Major typically involves 3-5+ years of intensive work before advancement to Exemptus is attempted.
