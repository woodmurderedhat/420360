Temperance

XIV
Upright Meanings:

• Balance
• Healing
• Moderation
• Blending
• Integration

Reversed Meanings:

• Excess
• Imbalance
• Immoderation
• Conflict
• Fragmentation

Element: Fire
Planet: Sagittarius
Color: Blue & Orange
Hebrew Letter: Samekh
Sephira: Yesoid/Tiphareth axis
Tarot Path: Between Yesoid & Tiphareth

Temperance is the alchemist's art: taking seemingly opposing elements and blending them into harmonious wholeness. In Hermetic philosophy, this represents the marriage of opposites—spiritual and material, conscious and unconscious, fire and water—creating medicine from potential poisons. Sagittarius seeks truth through exploration, and Temperance teaches that true wisdom lies not in extremes but in the balanced integration of all parts of self. The card appears when healing occurs through moderation and when seemingly incompatible forces are revealed as complementary.

Meditation Practice: The Alchemical Blend

Duration: 12 minutes

Best Time: During healing or when seeking balance

  1. Visualize yourself as an alchemist in a laboratory of light
  2. Identify two "opposing" aspects of yourself (logic/intuition, work/rest, etc.)
  3. In each hand, hold a vial representing each opposing force
  4. Slowly pour both vials into a golden bowl between your hands
  5. Watch the liquids blend into a new color—medicine, not poison
  6. Understand: integration heals what separation creates
  7. Drink this medicine, feeling wholeness flow through you

Integration: Seek balance not by suppressing either side, but by blending them intelligently. Wholeness requires all the colors of the spectrum.
