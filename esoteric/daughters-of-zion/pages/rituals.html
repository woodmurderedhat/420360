The Sevenfold Veil Initiation

The central ritual of the Order is the journey through the Seven Veils. This is not a single ceremony but a lifelong path of transformation, marked by specific rites at each stage:

1

Dust Initiation

The initiate sits in dust or earth, acknowledging her mortality and origin. She speaks her birth name and accepts her humble beginning.

2

Ash Initiation

The initiate marks herself with ash and speaks the names of those she has lost. She learns the Dance of Ash and joins the mourning circle.

3

Water Initiation

The initiate is washed by the Circle Mothers, symbolically releasing old patterns. She emerges renewed and speaks her intention to transform.

4

Oil Initiation

The initiate is anointed on forehead, hands, and feet. She receives her calling and commits to her sacred work within the community.

5

Wine Initiation

The initiate shares wine with the circle, celebrating her journey. She learns the Dance of Wine and embraces joy as sacred practice.

6

Milk Initiation

The initiate commits to nurturing others, whether through motherhood, teaching, or care. She receives the blessing of sustenance.

7

Light Initiation

The initiate receives her Hidden Name in a ceremony held at night. She lights her own lamp and becomes a full member of the Order.
