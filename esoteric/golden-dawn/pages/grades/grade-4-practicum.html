Golden Dawn Study

Philosophus (4=7) Practice Workbook

Daily Foundation Practices

Time Requirement: 45-60 minutes daily

  • LBRP (5 minutes)
  • Middle Pillar Exercise (15 minutes)
  • Invoking Watchtower Opening (20 minutes)
  • HGA Communion meditation (10-20 minutes)
Exercise 1: The Knowledge and Conversation

Purpose: Establish direct, conscious communication with your Holy Guardian Angel.

Practice: Daily, after the Middle Pillar, sit in silence and speak directly to your HGA: "Holy Guardian Angel, I am here. Reveal yourself to me. Guide me. I listen." Then listen—not with ears, but with inner awareness. Record all impressions in a journal. This is the central practice of Philosophus grade.

Exercise 2: Crossing the Abyss

Purpose: Prepare consciousness for the crossing from Ruach (lower self) to Neshamah (Higher Self).

Practice: Meditate on the Abyss—the vast emptiness that separates lower from higher consciousness. Understand this is not dangerous but necessary. Visualize yourself standing at the edge, expanding your awareness beyond all limitation. Perform this meditation 2x weekly for 20 minutes.

Exercise 3: Tiphareth Consciousness

Purpose: Attain consciousness of Tiphareth—the unified center-self.

Practice: Visualize the golden sphere of Tiphareth at your heart blazing with infinite light. Understand: "I am not body, not emotion, not thought—I am the eternal Self that witnesses all." Sit in this awareness for 15-20 minutes daily. This is enlightenment practice.

Exercise 4: The Diamond Body

Purpose: Crystallize your spiritual body into permanent form.

Practice: After the Middle Pillar, visualize yourself as a diamond-like crystal body radiating light. This body is incorruptible and eternal. Feel yourself gradually becoming this crystalline form through your daily practice. This represents the transformation of matter into spirit.

Weekly Assignments (40 Days)

Weeks 1-2: HGA Recognition

  • Study Tarot card The Sun (XIX) deeply
  • Perform HGA communion meditation daily
  • Study the Sephira Tiphareth thoroughly
  • Journal: "What messages have I received from my HGA?"

Weeks 3-4: Abyss Navigation

  • Study the Abyss and Daat extensively
  • Perform Abyss crossing meditation 2-3x weekly
  • Study mystical literature on enlightenment
  • Journal: "How is my consciousness evolving?"

Weeks 5-6: Unification

  • Study all higher sephiroth: Chokmah, Binah, Kether
  • Intensify HGA communion—aim for clear, undeniable contact
  • Perform the Portal Ritual if ready
  • Journal: "Am I ready for Adepthood?"
Exercise 5: The Unity Prayer

Purpose: Invoke the highest aspects of Divine consciousness.

Practice: Daily, after raising your hands to the heavens, speak:

"I am the Child of Light. I am the expression of the Divine. There is no separation between myself and the Infinite. I am One with All. All is One with me. So it is. So it shall be. Amen."

Philosophus Completion Exam (Day 40)
  • Documentation of all HGA communications
  • Complete understanding of Tiphareth consciousness
  • Experiences of the Abyss and what lies beyond
  • Evidence of expanded awareness and spiritual integration
  • Your vision for your future on the path
  • Formal readiness for Adeptus Minor grade

Next Step: Begin intensive preparation for the Abyss crossing or seek an Adeptus Minor teacher.
