Golden Dawn Ceremonial Rituals

Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Hexagram (LBRH)

Purpose: Banish planetary and zodiacal energies that may be influencing your space or consciousness using the six-pointed Star of David pattern.
Duration: 10-15 minutes
Grade: Intermediate (Practicus level)
Best Time: Morning, or when needing to clear planetary influences

Preparation

  • Face East (toward the rising sun)
  • Clear your space of distractions
  • You may use a wand, staff, or hand
  • Establish a calm, focused state of mind
  • Visualize brilliant white light surrounding you
1 Center of Force

Stand in the center of your space. Take three deep breaths. Feel yourself as the axis of the cosmos.

2 The Hexagrams of Banishment

Trace a large hexagram in the air before you, beginning at the bottom left and drawing to the top right. As you draw, visualize brilliant white or golden light forming the star. Repeat this hexagram in each direction: East, South, West, North.

"I banish all planetary and zodiacal influences that do not serve the Light!"
3 Invocation of the Archangels

At each quarter, invoke the appropriate planetary archangel:

East: "Taphthartharath! Mercury, Messenger of the Gods!"
South: "Khamael! Mars, Strength and Power!"
West: "Taphthartharath! Venus, Love and Harmony!"
North: "Uriel! Earth, Stability and Foundation!"
4 Sealing the Circle

Return to the center. Trace a large hexagram above your head, then one below your feet. Visualize yourself surrounded in a crystalline sphere of pure light.

5 The Middle Pillar Energy

Visualize white light descending from above through your crown, heart, and down through your feet into the earth. This anchors the banishment in physical reality.

6 Closing Statement

Cross your arms over your heart and speak with authority:

"By the power of the Hexagram and the authority of the Divine Names, this space is cleansed and protected. Only light, love, and truth may enter here. So it is."
7 Grounding

Take three more deep breaths. Stomp your feet gently to ground yourself in physical reality. The ritual is complete.

Important Notes

  • Visualization: The hexagrams must be seen as brilliant, glowing, and powerful. Your mental image makes them real on the astral plane.
  • Planetary Alignment: The hexagram has both upper (fire/light) and lower (water/form) triangles interlaced, representing the union of opposites.
  • Frequency: Use daily or weekly as needed. More powerful than the pentagram ritual for specific planetary work.
  • Variations: Can be used to invoke specific planetary energy (rotate the hexagram to emphasize specific points).
  • Duration: The more time you spend visualizing and feeling each step, the more powerful the ritual becomes.

After the Ritual

You should feel a noticeable shift in the quality of the space and your own consciousness. The air may feel clearer, lighter, more vibrant. This is the sign of successful work. Record your experience in your magical journal.
