The Star

XVII
Upright Meanings:

• Hope
• Guidance
• Clarity
• Inspiration
• Spiritual direction

Reversed Meanings:

• Lost hope
• Despair
• Lost direction
• Disconnection
• Disillusionment

Element: Air
Planet: Aquarius
Color: Bright Blue
Hebrew Letter: Tzade
Sephira: Netzach/Malkuth
Tarot Path: Between Netzach & Malkuth

After the Tower's devastation comes the Star—the light that guides through darkness. In Hermetic tradition, the star represents divine consciousness glimpsed directly, without filter or intermediary. Aquarius brings the vision of what is possible, the revolutionary dream. The Star teaches that even in the darkest night, there is always a light to follow—your true north, your deepest purpose. This is not the Priestess's hidden knowledge but revelation: you see directly what you must become. The card appears when hope is most needed and most true.

Meditation Practice: Following Your Star

Duration: 12 minutes

Best Time: When lost or seeking direction

  1. Lie on your back, seeing the night sky above you
  2. One star shines brighter than all others—this is your guiding star
  3. This star represents your truest purpose and highest self
  4. Feel its light on your face, in your heart
  5. Ask: "What is my star calling me toward?"
  6. Trust the answer that arises, whether dramatic or subtle
  7. Feel the hope of knowing you are never lost if you follow your star
  8. Commit to moving toward it, step by step

Integration: You carry a star within you. Follow it even when the path is unclear. It will never lead you astray, only toward your authentic destiny.
