Golden Dawn Ceremonial Rituals

Invoking Pentagram Ritual

Purpose: Call forth elemental and divine forces to aid in manifestation, protection, and empowerment. Used after banishing to bring desired energies into your sphere.
Duration: 10-15 minutes
Grade: Intermediate (Practicus level)
Best Time: When initiating projects, during waxing moon, morning hours

Preparation

  • First perform the Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram to clear the space
  • Face East (direction of Air and new beginnings)
  • Establish clear intention for what you wish to invoke
  • Visualize brilliant white light surrounding you
  • Feel your connection to the divine and elemental forces
1 Establish Center

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Feel yourself rooted and centered. Take three deep breaths, each time expanding your consciousness outward.

2 The Invoking Pentagram - East (Air)

Face East. Trace a pentagram in the air, beginning at the TOP and moving to the bottom-left, then to the right, back to top-right, bottom-left, and back to top. The energy should flow with power and intention.

"The Powers of Air attend! Intelligence and Clarity manifest!"
3 The Invoking Pentagram - South (Fire)

Turn South. Trace the invoking pentagram with even greater intensity, feeling heat and transformative power flowing through you.

"The Powers of Fire attend! Will and Transformation manifest!"
4 The Invoking Pentagram - West (Water)

Turn West. Trace the pentagram, feeling emotional depth, intuition, and flowing compassion.

"The Powers of Water attend! Love and Healing manifest!"
5 The Invoking Pentagram - North (Earth)

Turn North. Trace the pentagram with grounded, stable energy, bringing all manifestation into solid physical reality.

"The Powers of Earth attend! Manifestation and Stability manifest!"
6 Center of Power

Return to center. Trace a pentagram of brilliant white or gold light above your head and below your feet. Visualize yourself crowned with the four archangelic energies.

7 Statement of Invocation

With absolute conviction and power, speak your statement of intention:

"By the Four Elements and the Divine Powers, I invoke [your specific intention]. This work is done. By the authority vested in me, let it be manifest. So it is!"
8 Integration

Feel the invoked forces flowing through your being. They are not external anymore but woven into your own consciousness and energy field. Close with a final grounding breath.

Key Differences: Invoking vs. Banishing

  • Direction: Banishing starts bottom-left; Invoking starts at top
  • Energy: Invoking feels expansive, drawing power IN; Banishing feels repulsive, pushing power OUT
  • Intent: Invoking calls forces to aid you; Banishing removes unwanted influences
  • Timing: Always banish before invoking to ensure clean space
  • Visualization: Invoking pentagrams glow and pulse with active energy

After the Ritual

You should feel energized, purposeful, and aligned with your intention. The invoked forces will work throughout your day and beyond. Continue to act in alignment with your stated intention—ritual opens the door, but your actions walk through it.
