The Three Stations of Flame Meditation

First Station: Witnessing

Simply observe the flame without thought. Watch how it moves, how it bends with invisible currents. Your mind, like the flame, is always in motion. Can you observe it without trying to control it?

Second Station: Alignment

As you watch, feel the flame's light reaching toward truth. Feel yourself drawn upward with it. You are not separate from the fire‚Äîyour consciousness is also made of the same luminous essence.

Third Station: Integration

The flame is now within you. Its light illuminates every corner of your being. From this place of clarity, you understand: The fire never burns what is true. Only falseness is consumed. This is the gift of the Sacred Flame.
