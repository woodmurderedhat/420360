Golden Dawn Study

Theoricus (2=9) Practice Workbook

Daily Foundation Practices

Time Requirement: 25-35 minutes daily

  • LBRP (5 minutes)
  • Middle Pillar Exercise (10 minutes)
  • Elemental Invocation Pentagrams (East, South, West, North) (10 minutes)
  • Meditation on rational mind (5 minutes)
Exercise 1: The Four Elements Mastery

Purpose: Develop conscious command over all four elemental forces through invocation.

Practice: Each day, perform the Invoking Pentagram for each element:

  • East (Air): "By Raphael and the Powers of Air, I invoke clarity and intellect!"
  • South (Fire): "By Michael and the Powers of Fire, I invoke will and power!"
  • West (Water): "By Gabriel and the Powers of Water, I invoke feeling and compassion!"
  • North (Earth): "By Uriel and the Powers of Earth, I invoke manifestation and grounding!"

Exercise 2: The Path of Consciousness

Purpose: Understand the path from Yesoid (astral) to Hod (intellect/Mercury).

Practice: Meditate on this path 3x weekly. Visualize yourself rising from the violet sphere (Yesoid/Moon) to the orange sphere (Hod/Mercury). Understand that your rational mind is a tool of spiritual development, not an obstacle.

Exercise 3: Sacred Geometry Study

Purpose: Comprehend the mathematical patterns underlying creation.

Practice: Study and draw:

  • The Pentagram (5-pointed star) and its meanings
  • The Hexagram (6-pointed star) and its meanings
  • The Four Squares (representing the four elements)
  • The Tree of Life and all its paths
Spend 30 minutes weekly on geometrical drawing and contemplation.

Exercise 4: The Divine Names

Purpose: Master the Sacred Names corresponding to each sephira.

Practice: Memorize and meditate on each Divine Name:

  • Malkuth: Adonai Ha-Aretz (Lord of Earth)
  • Yesoid: Shaddai El Chai (Almighty Living God)
  • Hod: Elohim Tzabaoth (God of Hosts)
  • Netzach: Jehovah Tzabaoth (Lord of Hosts)
  • Tiphareth: Jehovah Aloah Va-Daath (God of Knowledge)
Vibrate these names aloud while meditating on their corresponding sephiroth.

Weekly Assignments (40 Days)

Weeks 1-2: Elemental Mastery

  • Study Tarot cards 8-14 (Strength through Temperance)
  • Perform Elemental Invoking Pentagrams daily
  • Meditate on each archangel individually: Michael, Gabriel, Raphael, Uriel
  • Journal: "How do the four elements manifest in my personality?"

Weeks 3-4: Hod Consciousness

  • Study Mercury (Hod) correspondences thoroughly
  • Practice the Path of Consciousness meditation 4x weekly
  • Study sacred geometry and draw daily
  • Journal: "What is my relationship with rational thought and intellect?"

Weeks 5-6: Integration

  • Study all 32 paths of the Tree of Life
  • Perfect your understanding of Hermetic correspondences
  • Perform a full Elemental Opening (all four directions) daily
  • Journal: "How has my mental clarity deepened through magical practice?"
Exercise 5: The Caduceus Meditation

Purpose: Balance masculine (will) and feminine (intuition) aspects of consciousness.

Practice: Visualize the Caduceus (Mercury's staff with two serpents): one red (will), one blue (intuition), spiraling around a central rod (consciousness). Meditate that these two forces flow through you in perfect balance. Perform weekly.

Theoricus Completion Exam (Day 40)

Write comprehensive answers:

  • All correspondences for Mercury and Hod
  • The function and form of each Elemental Invoking Pentagram
  • How your understanding of sacred geometry has evolved
  • Your experiences of clarity and mental illumination
  • How the four elements work through your personality
  • Your readiness to balance intellect with emotion (Practicus)
