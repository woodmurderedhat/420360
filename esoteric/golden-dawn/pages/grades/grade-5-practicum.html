Golden Dawn Study

Adeptus Minor (5=6) Practice Workbook

Daily Foundation Practices

Time Requirement: 60-90 minutes daily

  • Complete Banishing Ritual (LBRP + Watchtower) (20 minutes)
  • Middle Pillar with extended circulation (20 minutes)
  • Portal Ritual or advanced HGA work (25-30 minutes)
  • Contemplative meditation on unity (15-20 minutes)
Exercise 1: The Red Wand Practice

Purpose: Become adept at wielding magical will for manifestation and healing.

Practice: Craft or purchase a red wand or rod. Consecrate it with the four elements. Use it daily to direct energy for healing work, creative projects, or spiritual protection. The wand becomes an extension of your will. Practice fine control: directing energy to specific people, places, or situations. Record all magical workings.

Exercise 2: Abyss Mastery

Purpose: Consciously cross the Abyss and integrate superconsciousness.

Practice: Perform the Portal Ritual (crossing into consciousness beyond the Abyss) 2-3 times per month. Each crossing deepens your attainment. Keep detailed journal of experiences. The Abyss will teach you directly what you need to know. Surrender to its wisdom.

Exercise 3: The Great Work of Perfection

Purpose: Begin the Great Work—transmuting yourself into an instrument of Divine will.

Practice: Identify one character flaw or shadow aspect of yourself. Work magically to transmute it. This is not denial—it's integration and transformation. Spend 40+ days working to heal this aspect through magical practice, meditation, and conscious living. Then work on the next aspect. This is lifetime work.

Exercise 4: Teaching and Service

Purpose: Recognize that spiritual advancement requires service to others.

Practice: Begin teaching Hermetic philosophy, even informally. Help others on the path. Offer healing work. Share what you've learned. The Adept is a servant of humanity. This practice grounds your spirituality in compassionate action.

Weekly Assignments (Ongoing)

Monthly Practices:

  • Portal Ritual (Abyss crossing): 2-3 times per month
  • Advanced healing or creative magic: 1-2 major workings weekly
  • Study advanced Hermetic texts: 5+ hours weekly
  • Service work or teaching: Ongoing and regular

Quarterly Practices:

  • Retreat day: 8-12 hours intensive magical work and meditation
  • Complete review of all grades: Restudy each grade's correspondences
  • Update magical journal with overall progress
Exercise 5: The Rose and Cross Union

Purpose: Perfect balance of love and will, emotion and intellect, heart and mind.

Practice: Daily meditation: Visualize the Rose (Netzach—love, beauty, emotion) perfectly united with the Cross (Hod—order, intellect, will). These are not opposites—they are two aspects of the same sacred power. Feel this union crystallizing in your consciousness and heart. This is the attainment of Adeptus.

Adeptus Minor Ongoing Development

Key Practices:

  • Regular Abyss crossing work (Portal Ritual)
  • Development of magical powers for healing and helping
  • Continuous study and integration of Hermetic philosophy
  • Service to humanity and to the Divine
  • Perfection of character through conscious magical transformation
  • Preparation for the highest grades (Adeptus Major and Exemptus)

Timeline: Adeptus Minor is a grade of ongoing development. Spend 1-3+ years here before attempting advancement to Adeptus Major.
