Golden Dawn Study

Neophyte (0=0) Practice Workbook

Daily Foundation Practices

Time Requirement: 15-20 minutes daily

  • Perform the Qabalistic Cross (5 minutes)
  • Perform the LBRP (5-10 minutes)
  • Meditation: "I am a child of the Divine" (5 minutes)
Exercise 1: The Qabalistic Cross

Purpose: Ground yourself in the divine source and establish the connection between heaven and earth.

Practice: Perform this exercise before each daily ritual. With your right hand, touch your forehead and say "Atah" (Thou art). Touch your chest and say "Malkuth" (the Kingdom). Touch your right shoulder and say "ve-Geburah" (and the Power). Touch your left shoulder and say "ve-Gedulah" (and the Glory). Clasp hands at chest and say "le-olam, Amen" (forever, Amen).

Repetitions: 7 times daily for 40 days

Exercise 2: Grounding Technique

Purpose: Anchor your consciousness firmly in the physical body and material reality.

Practice: Sit with both feet flat on the ground. Visualize roots growing from your feet deep into the earth. Feel the solidity of the ground beneath you. Say: "I am grounded. I am present. I am here." Repeat 3-5 times.

When to Use: Whenever you feel spacey, anxious, or disconnected. Also before any magical work.

Exercise 3: Elemental Awareness

Purpose: Develop sensitivity to the four elements within and around you.

Practice: Each day, focus on one element:

  • Monday (Air): Notice the air moving on your skin, around you. Feel the element of thought and clarity.
  • Tuesday (Fire): Observe fire (candle, sun). Feel the element of will and passion within you.
  • Wednesday (Water): Touch water. Feel the element of emotion and intuition flowing.
  • Thursday (Earth): Touch soil, stone, earth. Feel the element of manifestation and grounding.

Weekly Assignments (40 Days)

Weeks 1-2: Foundation

  • Study the Tree of Life diagram daily
  • Memorize the 4 Qabalistic Worlds: Atziluth, Briah, Yetzirah, Assiah
  • Perform LBRP twice daily (morning and evening)
  • Journal: "What does 'the Great Work' mean to me?"

Weeks 3-4: Deepening

  • Study the four elements and their correspondences
  • Practice the Qabalistic Cross 7 times daily
  • Meditation focus: "I am the Child of Divine Light"
  • Journal: "What does initiation mean to me?"

Weeks 5-6: Integration

  • Study the four archangels: Michael, Gabriel, Raphael, Uriel
  • Elemental invocations: Call to each direction once daily
  • Extended grounding practice (10 minutes daily)
  • Journal: "What divine qualities do I wish to develop?"
Exercise 4: The Temple of the Mind

Purpose: Develop your inner sanctuary—a place within consciousness where you can retreat for work and reflection.

Practice: Close your eyes. Visualize a simple structure: a temple, a garden, a chamber, or sacred space that feels safe and beautiful to you. Walk around it. Explore it. Establish it as your inner sanctum. Practice entering this space through meditation 3-4 times per week for the full 40 days. It will become increasingly vivid and real.

Final Neophyte Examination (Day 40)

Purpose: Assess readiness for advancement.

Write out in your journal:

  • The names and functions of the four archangels
  • The four worlds and what they represent
  • The ten sephiroth and their basic meanings
  • Your experiences with meditation and magical practice
  • How your understanding of the Divine has evolved
  • Your intention for the next stage of the path

Next Step: If you feel called to continue, perform the Zelator Initiation Ritual.
