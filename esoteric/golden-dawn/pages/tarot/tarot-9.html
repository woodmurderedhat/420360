Strength is not about force, but about the ability to remain centered in your power without needing to assert it aggressively. In Hermetic tradition, true strength comes from understanding your will and channeling it with compassion. Leo energy is regal and confident, yet the card teaches that real power lies in gentleness combined with unwavering resolve. It's the strength to maintain your boundaries without harming others, to speak truth without violence, to be soft yet unbreakable. This is the strength of the initiate who has mastered the self.

Meditation Practice: The Lion's Heart

Duration: 10 minutes

Best Time: When facing fear or need to stand firm

  1. Sit with your heart center open and exposed
  2. Visualize a golden lion before you, powerful yet peaceful
  3. The lion looks at you with ancient wisdom and compassion
  4. It approaches and places its great head over your heart
  5. Feel the lion's strength merging with your own essence
  6. Know that true power requires no aggression
  7. Speak from this place: "I am strong, steady, and at peace"
  8. Feel the lion's presence as a mantle of protection

Integration: Real strength is quiet confidence. It doesn't need to prove itself. Develop unshakeable resolve combined with genuine compassion.
