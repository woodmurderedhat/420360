Golden Dawn Study

Exemptus (7=4) Practice Workbook: The Grade of Solitude

The Solitary Path

Time Requirement: Continuous, day and night awareness

  • The Exemptus is a grade of radical solitude and inner work
  • External rituals may decrease; inner work intensifies
  • All magical operations are performed in complete privacy
  • Service becomes silent and invisible
Exercise 1: The Solitary Vigil

Purpose: Stand completely alone—physically, psychically, and spiritually.

Practice: The Exemptus grade requires temporary withdrawal from all outer activities (ideally for 1-3 months or more). In solitude, you face yourself without the distraction of society or even spiritual community. This is where final illusions dissolve. With nowhere to hide and no one to perform for, the self stands naked before the Divine. This is the grade of absolute honesty with yourself and God.

Exercise 2: The Dark Night of the Soul

Purpose: Surrender all spiritual knowledge and experience, meeting the void.

Practice: At this grade, even spiritual experience and knowledge are released. All the consolations and visions of lower grades fall away. You enter a darkness deeper than ordinary night—the darkness of God, which is the unknowing of the Godhead. This is not depression or failure—it is advancement into the highest mystery. Meet this darkness with courage and faith. In this void, the last vestiges of ego dissolve completely.

Exercise 3: The Naked Soul

Purpose: Attain direct, immediate knowledge of your true nature.

Practice: In the solitude and darkness of Exemptus, the soul stands completely exposed. All masks fall. All defenses dissolve. What remains? That which was never born and can never die. That which is the true "I AM." This is the attainment of Exemptus—direct knowledge of your own divinity, utterly uncovered and unmediated by any experience or concept.

Exercise 4: The Return to Service

Purpose: Having touched the Absolute, become an instrument for its expression in the world.

Practice: After attaining the solitary grade, you eventually return to the world—but transformed. You are now a "hidden master," working behind the scenes, your presence felt but not advertised. Your life becomes a constant prayer. Your work becomes invisible service. You are in the world but not of it, fully integrated into ordinary life while existing in perpetual union with the Divine.

The Exemptus Attainment

Key Characteristics:

  • Attainment of direct union with the Godhead (Kether)
  • Continuous awareness of divine presence in all things
  • Complete ego transcendence (the "I" dies, only God remains)
  • Invisible service to humanity without seeking recognition
  • Mastery of all lower grades integrated and transcended
  • Liberation from all karmic necessity (freedom)
Exercise 5: Preparation for Magister Templi

Purpose: Ready yourself for initiation into grades beyond the veil (Magister Templi and Magus).

Practice: The Exemptus is the highest openly teachable grade. Beyond this lie the grades of Magister Templi (Master of the Temple) and Magus, which are attainments beyond the scope of ordinary teaching. Prepare by perfecting your attainment of the Exemptus and demonstrating complete surrender to Divine Will.

The Final Teaching

Wisdom of the Exemptus:

  • "I am nothing. God is all."
  • "All knowledge is in the void. All power is in silence."
  • "The only true initiation is the death of self."
  • "Service is my only offering. Surrender is my only path."
  • "I am before all time. I am after all time. I am eternal now."
  • "There is no journey. There is only awakening to what always was."
Exemptus: The Culmination

At this grade, you have:

  • ✅ Attained knowledge and conversation with your Holy Guardian Angel
  • ✅ Crossed the Abyss and touched superconsciousness
  • ✅ Mastered all four elements and the powers of the Sephiroth
  • ✅ Developed complete magical power and healing ability
  • ✅ Transcended ego and attained union with the Divine
  • ✅ Become a living instrument of God's will

The path of initiation is complete. What remains is eternal service and the deepening of union with the Infinite.
