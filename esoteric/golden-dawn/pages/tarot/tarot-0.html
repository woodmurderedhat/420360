The journey begins

The Fool stands at the edge of a cliff, about to step off into the unknown. Behind him lies the world of certainty and tradition. Before him lies infinite possibility. He carries his possessions on a stick‚Äîeverything he needs is portable, everything he values can be released.

The Fool is you at the beginning of your journey. The moment you decide to seek truth, to explore the mysteries, to step beyond the boundaries of conventional understanding. This is the moment of greatest power because it contains infinite potential.

The Fool's Journey

In the context of the Major Arcana, The Fool represents the eternal seeker. All the other 21 cards represent stages on the Fool's path through the mysteries. From innocent Neophyte to enlightened Master, the Fool journeys through all the grades and experiences.

The Fool's task is not to become someone else, but to awaken to who you really are. This journey requires:

Meditation Practice

When you draw The Fool in meditation or divination, ask yourself: What am I being called to start? What conventions am I being asked to release? Where is my innocence and openness being invited forth?

The Fool teaches that the greatest wisdom often looks like foolishness to the world. But true foolishness is thinking you know everything already. The Fool knows he knows nothing, and that is why he is about to learn everything.

Begin here. Begin now. Begin again.
