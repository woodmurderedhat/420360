Meditation Practice: The Union of Opposites

Duration: 12 minutes

Best Time: When facing major decisions or relationship challenges

  4. Step toward the mirror and unite with this reflection
  5. Feel the wholeness of being aligned with yourself
  6. From this place of integrity, visualize a choice before you
