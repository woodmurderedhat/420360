The Wheel of Fortune represents the eternal turning of fate, the cycles of rise and fall that govern all existence. In Hermetic tradition, the Wheel teaches that all things change‚Äîwhat rises must fall, what falls shall rise again. Jupiter's influence brings expansion and opportunity, but also the reminder that fortune is never permanent. The wise initiate learns to remain centered at the hub of the wheel, unmoved by the turning of circumstances. The four figures at the wheel's corners represent the fixed signs of the zodiac, the stable forces within constant change.

Meditation Practice: The Eternal Wheel

  2. See figures rising and falling as the wheel rotates
  3. Now see yourself moving toward the center‚Äîthe hub
  4. At the center, feel perfect stillness while the wheel turns
  5. From here, you can observe all change without being thrown
  6. Understand: circumstances change, but your true self remains
  7. Accept both rise and fall as teachers, not masters
