Meditation Practice: The Garden of Becoming

Duration: 12 minutes

Best Time: Spring or during waxing moon

  1. Visualize yourself in a lush garden full of blooming flowers
  2. Feel the fertile earth beneath your feet, rich with possibility
  3. Place your hands on your heart center
  4. With each exhale, imagine creative energy flowing from your heart into the garden
  5. Watch as new flowers bloom where your energy touches
  6. Ask: "What seeks to be created through me?"
  7. Receive the answer as a seed of light planted in your heart

Integration: Notice what wants to grow in your life. Support it with consistent attention and nurture, like a gardener tends growing plants.
