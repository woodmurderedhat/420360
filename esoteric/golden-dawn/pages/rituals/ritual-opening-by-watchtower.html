Golden Dawn Ceremonial Rituals

Opening by Watchtower

Purpose: Invoke the complete Watchtower hierarchy (Archangels, Kings, Princes, and Elementals) to establish a fully protected and empowered sacred space for high magic work.
Duration: 20-30 minutes
Grade: Practicus level
Best Time: For major workings, rituals, or group ceremony

Preparation

  • Begin with the Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram (LBRP)
  • Establish yourself at the center of your space
  • Face East
  • Have your magical instruments ready (wand, sword, cup, pentacle optional)
  • Invoke the Archangel Michael in the East before beginning
1 Raise Divine Consciousness

Visualize white light descending from above through your crown, filling your entire being with divine presence.

2 Invoke the Four Archangels at Quarters

East (Air): "Mighty Raphael, Archangel of Air, fill this space with divine intelligence and clarity!"
South (Fire): "Mighty Michael, Archangel of Fire, fill this space with divine will and power!"
West (Water): "Mighty Gabriel, Archangel of Water, fill this space with divine love and compassion!"
North (Earth): "Mighty Uriel, Archangel of Earth, fill this space with divine strength and grounding!"

3 Invoke the Four Elemental Kings

East: "King Paralda rules the Eastern Air. I invoke thee!"
South: "King Djin rules the Southern Fire. I invoke thee!"
West: "King Nichsa rules the Western Water. I invoke thee!"
North: "King Ghob rules the Northern Earth. I invoke thee!"

4 Call the Watchtowers

Raise your hands and speak with authority:

"I call upon the ancient Watchtowers, upon all celestial and terrestrial powers, visible and invisible. I call upon the Divine Source itself. By the authority granted me, I declare this space consecrated, protected, and empowered for the Great Work. Let all powers of light attend!"

5 Center of Power

Visualize yourself standing at the center of concentric circles of light—elementals, kings, princes, and archangels all circling you in perfect formation, utterly obedient to divine will.

6 Statement of Work

Declare your magical purpose with absolute clarity and conviction. The Watchtower now supports your work.

7 Closing the Watchtower

When your work is complete, thank each Watchtower and dismiss them:

"Mighty Raphael, Paralda, and all Powers of Air, I license you to depart. Hail and Farewell!"
Repeat for each direction. Perform the Banishing Ritual to close completely.

The Watchtower Hierarchy

  • Archangels: Divine representatives, immediate presence of the Godhead
  • Kings: Rulership of entire elemental realms
  • Princes: Authority within specific regions or sub-elements
  • Elementals: Individual spirits that carry out the work

After the Opening

You should feel an unmistakable presence of power and protection. The space itself should feel different—alive, sacred, and completely under your command. Use this opening for important work only.
