Golden Dawn Study

Zelator (1=10) Practice Workbook

Daily Foundation Practices

Time Requirement: 20-30 minutes daily

  • LBRP (10 minutes)
  • Middle Pillar Exercise (10 minutes)
  • Earth element grounding meditation (5 minutes)
Exercise 1: Earth Element Mastery

Purpose: Ground yourself in physical manifestation and understand matter as sacred.

Practice: Daily, hold a stone or earth in your hands. Visualize it glowing with divine light. Understand that "matter" is Spirit condensed. Affirm: "All manifestation is Divine. The physical world is sacred. I am rooted in both heaven and earth."

Duration: 40 days of daily practice

Exercise 2: Banishing Mastery

Purpose: Perfect the LBRP until it becomes second nature.

Practice: Perform the LBRP twice daily. By day 20, it should take 8-10 minutes. By day 40, 5-7 minutes. By the end, the ritual flows naturally without conscious thought.

Advanced technique: Perform the LBRP while walking in a circle in your home, establishing a permanent protective boundary around your space.

Exercise 3: The Astral Body Awareness

Purpose: Develop awareness of your astral (emotional/energetic) body.

Practice: Before sleep, lie in bed and visualize a duplicate of yourself made of light standing beside your physical body. Say: "I have an astral body. It is real. It moves with my will." Practice 3-4 times per week. Do not force astral projection yet—just develop the awareness.

Exercise 4: The Temple Doorway

Purpose: Establish a gateway between your physical temple (home) and your astral temple (inner planes).

Practice: In your home, designate a specific doorway or location as your "temple gateway." When passing through it, perform a micro-banishing (quick sign of the cross). Say: "I enter sacred space. I am between worlds." This trains your consciousness that certain locations are thresholds to other dimensions.

Weekly Assignments (40 Days)

Weeks 1-2: Earth Grounding

  • Journal: Study and contemplate each Tarot card 0-7 (Fool through Chariot)
  • Spend 5 minutes daily meditating on physical sensations
  • Practice elemental invocations for North (Earth) daily
  • Record your dreams—they are astral experiences

Weeks 3-4: Banishing Mastery

  • Study the Archangel Michael (rules Yesoid, your astral home)
  • Perform LBRP three times daily if possible
  • Practice astral body awareness before bed
  • Journal: "What fears am I banishing from my consciousness?"

Weeks 5-6: Integration

  • Study Yesoid (Moon/Astral Planes) thoroughly
  • Perfect your Middle Pillar technique
  • Begin visualizing your temple doorway each day
  • Journal: "How has my relationship with the material world changed?"
Exercise 5: The Four Pillars Meditation

Purpose: Understand the balance of forces represented by the pillars of the Tree of Life.

Practice: Meditate on the three pillars:

  • Pillar of Severity (left): Divine judgment, boundaries, form
  • Pillar of Mildness (middle): Balance, equilibrium, the path
  • Pillar of Mercy (right): Divine compassion, expansion, consciousness
Contemplate how all three work together. Perform this meditation once weekly.

Zelator Completion Exam (Day 40)

Purpose: Assess readiness to cross to Hod (rational mind).

Write in your journal:

  • Detailed description of your astral body awareness
  • All planetary correspondences for the Moon (Yesoid)
  • The purpose and structure of the LBRP
  • How your grounding has deepened your magical practice
  • Your experiences crossing between physical and astral awareness
  • Your readiness to advance to Theoricus
