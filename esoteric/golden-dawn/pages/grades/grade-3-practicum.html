Golden Dawn Study

Practicus (3=8) Practice Workbook

Daily Foundation Practices

Time Requirement: 30-45 minutes daily

  • LBRP (5 minutes)
  • Middle Pillar Exercise (10 minutes)
  • Elemental Invoking Pentagrams (10 minutes)
  • Heart-centered meditation on love and beauty (10 minutes)
Exercise 1: Netzach Consciousness Expansion

Purpose: Open your heart to the creative and emotional powers of Netzach.

Practice: Visualize the emerald-green sphere of Netzach blazing in your heart center. Feel the seven rays of Venus emanating outward. Understand that you are a creative instrument of the Divine. Perform this meditation 3-4 times weekly for 15 minutes each.

Exercise 2: Astral Travel Preparation

Purpose: Develop the ability to consciously project your awareness to the astral planes.

Practice: Before sleep, lie in relaxed position. Visualize your astral body standing beside your physical form. Practice rising up in this astral form. Don't force—just suggest it gently to your consciousness. Record all experiences in a dream journal immediately upon waking.

Exercise 3: Creative Magic Practice

Purpose: Learn to manifest desires through creative visualization and magical will.

Practice: Choose one small, achievable goal (not materialistic—perhaps healing, clarity, or inspiration). Create a candle ritual:

  • Light a candle and visualize your goal as already manifest
  • State your intention aloud three times
  • Feel the emotion as if it's already true
  • Let the candle burn to completion
  • Release attachment to the outcome
Practice once weekly for the entire 40 days.

Exercise 4: The Path of Desire

Purpose: Understand the journey from intellect (Hod) to emotional-creative power (Netzach).

Practice: Meditate on this path 3x weekly. Visualize your consciousness moving from the orange sphere (Hod) to the emerald sphere (Netzach). Understand: "My intellect is balanced by my heart. My will is refined by love."

Weekly Assignments (40 Days)

Weeks 1-2: Heart Opening

  • Study Tarot cards 15-19 (Devil through Sun)
  • Meditate on Venus correspondences daily
  • Study the nature of love, beauty, and creative force
  • Journal: "What does beauty mean to me? How do I express creativity?"

Weeks 3-4: Astral Mastery

  • Practice astral body awareness nightly
  • Study the astral planes and their inhabitants
  • Create and perform a candle ritual weekly
  • Journal: "What astral experiences have I had?"

Weeks 5-6: Integration

  • Deepen astral travel practice
  • Study the Rose and Cross symbol (union of opposites)
  • Perform creative magic daily
  • Journal: "How have I become more balanced and whole?"
Exercise 5: The Spiral Dance

Purpose: Balance masculine will with feminine intuition through sacred movement.

Practice: In a safe space, slowly spiral clockwise while chanting: "I spin in the spiral of creation. Will and Love dance as One. I am balanced. I am whole." Perform for 10 minutes, 2-3 times weekly. This activates the energetic body.

Practicus Completion Exam (Day 40)
  • All Venus and Netzach correspondences
  • Your astral travel experiences and journal records
  • Documentation of magical workings performed
  • How your heart has opened through practice
  • Your experiences of creativity and inspiration
  • Readiness for the Knowledge and Conversation with HGA
